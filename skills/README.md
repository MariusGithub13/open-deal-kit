# II · The Diligence Skills

**50+ named tools, one job each.** Set one up, point it at a document, get the answer.

A skill is a single, repeatable job you can hand Claude in one line: *"Act as the Add-Back Auditor and classify every EBITDA adjustment in the attached financials as legitimate, arguable or aggressive, with the reason and the page."* Each carries the same rule, cite the source or say it isn't there.

Several of these are also available as fully installable, prompt-complete **[agents](../agents/)** (for example the Citation Checker, Customer-Concentration Scanner and Quick-Screen Scorer). Use the agent when you want the full guided prompt; use the skill name as a quick instruction when you already know what you want.

## Financial diligence
*12 skills*

| Skill | Does |
|-------|------|
| Add-Back Auditor | Classify every EBITDA adjustment as legitimate, arguable or aggressive, with the reason and the page. |
| Margin Tracker | Chart gross and operating margin across all periods and explain each movement from the data. |
| Working-Capital Estimator | Estimate the working capital needed to run the business for a year and how it's funded. |
| Cash-Conversion Checker | Test whether reported profit turns into cash, period by period, and flag any gap. |
| Revenue-Quality Grader | Grade revenue on recurrence, contract backing and concentration, with an evidenced score. |
| Capex-Time-Bomb Detector | Find deferred capex and aging assets that will demand cash the projections ignore. |
| One-Off Spotter | Identify non-recurring items inflating profit in any period. |
| Debt & Obligations Mapper | Extract all debt, leases and obligations a buyer would assume, with amounts and terms. |
| Quality-of-Earnings Lite | Produce a short QoE-style red-flag list from the financials provided. |
| Forecast Pressure-Tester | Expose the load-bearing assumptions in every projection and test how fragile each is. |
| Seasonality Mapper | Surface seasonality and period concentration in the numbers. |
| Lender's-Eye Reader | Read the financials as a lender would and list what they'd worry about. |

## Red flags & risk
*12 skills*

| Skill | Does |
|-------|------|
| Customer-Concentration Scanner | Quantify top-customer revenue share and flag the fragility, citing the data. |
| Owner-Dependence Detector | Measure how much the business depends on the owner and what walks at close. |
| Contradiction Finder | Surface every place the narrative and the numbers disagree, quoting both. |
| Footnote Landmine Hunter | Search the footnotes and fine print for the risks the headline hides. |
| Supplier-Dependence Checker | Flag single-supplier, single-channel or single-platform exposure. |
| Compliance-Exposure Flag | Identify regulatory, licensing or compliance risk referenced in the materials. |
| Churn-Signal Reader | Find evidence of churn or weakening retention in whatever data exists. |
| Marketing-vs-Fact Filter | Strip the marketing language and leave only verifiable claims. |
| Worst-Case Framer | Build the worst realistic reading of the deal using only cited facts. |
| Risk Ranker | Rank all identified risks by severity and by cost-to-verify. |
| Fragility Test | Assess whether the business is more fragile than its headline numbers suggest. |
| Dealbreaker Sorter | Split risks into dealbreakers vs price-adjustments. |

## Commercial & customer
*10 skills*

| Skill | Does |
|-------|------|
| Revenue-Model Mapper | Describe exactly how the business earns: recurring vs one-off, contracted vs at-will, cited. |
| Contract-Term Extractor | Tabulate each contract's term, value, renewal and termination rights. |
| Change-of-Control Scanner | Find every change-of-control and assignment clause that affects a buyer. |
| Customer-Stickiness Grader | Assess switching costs and stickiness from the available evidence. |
| Pipeline Reality-Checker | Test whether the stated pipeline or backlog is real and contracted. |
| Pricing-Power Reader | Assess whether the business can raise prices, from the evidence. |
| Competitor Mapper | Name the real competitors the CIM may be downplaying. |
| Market-Claim Auditor | Separate the seller's market claims (cited) from grounded estimates (labelled). |
| Concentration-by-Segment | Break concentration down by customer, product, channel and geography. |
| Retention Estimator | Estimate retention or repeat-rate from whatever customer data exists. |

*10 skills*

|-------|------|

## Decision & memo
*8 skills*

| Skill | Does |
|-------|------|
| Quick-Screen Scorer | Score a deal against my thesis in minutes, with reasoning and a go / hold / pass. |
| Bull/Bear Builder | Produce a cited bull case and bear case, side by side. |
| Cited-Brief Builder | Produce a full diligence brief where every claim points to its source page. |
| Citation Checker | Audit any brief against its source and list claims that can't be traced. |
| IC-Memo Builder | Assemble diligence into an IC-style memo, risks weighted equally to strengths. |

| Offer-Rationale Writer | Write a one-paragraph, defensible rationale for my price. |
| Red-Flag Summary | Produce the single-page red-flag summary for a deal. |
