# IV · The Systems

**4 systems, the methods that turn the tools into a repeatable way of working a deal.**

Prompts and skills handle single moments. Systems tie them together into a method you run the same way every time. Each is installable as a Claude skill (a `SKILL.md` you can drop in), or just copy the prompt.

| System | What it does |
|--------|--------------|
| [The Cited Brief System](cited-brief-system/SKILL.md) **· flagship** | Turn a CIM or data room into a structured brief where every claim cites its source page, ending with a "cannot verify" list. |
| [The Quick-Screen Scorecard](quick-screen-scorecard/SKILL.md) | Score any deal across nine criteria out of 18 the moment it lands: pursue, hold, or pass. |
| [The Diligence Question Bank](diligence-question-bank/SKILL.md) | The questions a ten-person deal team asks without thinking, by area, plus a prompt to tailor them to your deal. |
| [The IC Memo Builder](ic-memo-builder/SKILL.md) | Turn your diligence into a clean, honest IC-style memo, risks weighted equally to strengths, every figure cited. |

The flagship is the **Cited Brief System** paired with the **[Citation Checker](../agents/citation-checker/SKILL.md)**: produce a fully-cited brief, then audit it back against the source so nothing unverified slips into a decision. That pairing is the whole idea of the kit.
