# The Diligence Question Bank

*System 3 of 4 · THE QUESTIONS YOU'D FORGET · [The Deal Kit](../../README.md)*

The questions a ten-person deal team would ask without thinking, organised by area so nothing slips. Use them to build your management-meeting list and your information requests. The prompt at the end tailors the bank to your specific deal.

---

## The bank

**Financial**
- What's driving the revenue trend, volume, price, or new customers?
- Walk me through every add-back in adjusted EBITDA. Which survive a sale?
- How much owner compensation and personal expense is in these numbers?
- What capex is coming in the next two years that isn't in the forecast?
- How much working capital does the business need, and how seasonal is it?
- Does reported profit turn into cash, and where does the cash go?
- Have you restated or adjusted any prior-year figures? Why?
- Who prepares the accounts, and are they audited or reviewed?

**Commercial / customers**
- What share of revenue is the top customer? The top five?
- Which revenue is contracted vs at-will, and on what terms?
- What's your churn or repeat rate, and how is it measured?
- Have you lost any major customer in three years? Why?
- Do any contracts carry change-of-control or termination clauses?
- How much of the pipeline is committed vs hoped-for?
- Can you raise prices? When did you last, and what happened?
- Who are your real competitors, and where are you losing?

**Operations & people**
- What breaks if volume doubles? If it halves?
- Which systems, tools or suppliers is the business dependent on?
- What exactly do you, the owner, do day to day?
- What happens to the business the day after you leave?
- Who are the key people, are they staying, and on what terms?
- Who holds the key customer and supplier relationships?

**Legal & the deal**
- What licences or approvals does the business rely on?
- Any past, current or threatened litigation or disputes?
- Any contingent liabilities, guarantees or off-balance-sheet items?
- Is the IP owned, registered and assignable?
- Why are you selling, and why now?
- Are you open to an earn-out or seller note?
- What other parties looked, and why didn't it proceed?
- What's not in the data room that I should know about?
